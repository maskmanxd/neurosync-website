How does TMS Therapy work?

TMS therapy is a safe and effective outpatient treatment that utilizes magnetic pulses to stimulate inactive parts of the brain. It utilizes electromagnetic fields generated by specially designed coils to safely reach deep brain structures and influence neural activity. It is a non-invasive and non-pharmacological approach, often used when traditional antidepressant medications have failed to produce satisfactory results for the patient. TMS therapy does not require any surgical procedures or skin puncturing, and it is painless. Patients have reported only minor side effects, such as mild scalp discomfort at the treatment site and headaches, which are generally managed with over-the-counter pain medication. These side effects typically resolve within the first week of treatment.
