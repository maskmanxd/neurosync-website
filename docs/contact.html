ABOUT OUR OFFICE

University Place, WA is a beautiful city located in Pierce County, Washington. It has a lot to offer, including stunning views of Puget Sound and the Olympic Mountains, a variety of outdoor recreational activities, and a vibrant community of friendly and welcoming people.

To get to 4007 Bridgeport Way W, Suite B, University Place, WA 98466 from major routes, you can take the I-5 South to exit 132 for WA-16 West towards Bremerton. Follow WA-16 West for approximately 10 miles, then take the exit for Center Street/WA-166 towards South 19th Street. Turn left onto Center Street and continue for about 2 miles. Turn left onto Bridgeport Way West, and you will find 4007 Bridgeport Way W, Suite B on your left.

Overall, University Place is a great destination for anyone looking for a beautiful and welcoming community with plenty to see and do.
