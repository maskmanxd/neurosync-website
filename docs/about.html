About Us

TRANSFORMING MENTAL HEALTH CARE

NeuroSync TMS Therapy is dedicated to providing exceptional care and treatment for adults living with anxiety, depression, ADHD, and other mood disorders. We will partner with you to achieve your mental health goals so you can live an optimal life. Our team of highly trained, compassionate mental health professionals is committed to providing personalized care to each and every patient.

Empathy and compassion are vital components of effective mental health care. We strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment that empowers patients to prioritize their well-being. We partner with you on your journey toward improved mental health. Seeking help takes courage. You don't have to do it alone

Meet Our Specialist

Ayub Mwaura

MSN, PMHNP-BC

Ayub Mwaura, MSN, PMHNP-BC

I am a PMHNP-BC with over 16 years of psychiatric nursing experience, board-certified in Psychiatric Mental Health. I earned my BSN from Winston Salem State University and MSN & PMHNP from University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. My expertise includes inpatient psychiatric units, emergency rooms, long-term care facilities, and outpatient clinics. I specialize in treating adults with mental illnesses such as anxiety, depression, ADHD, and other mood disorders, utilizing TMS, medication management, and supportive psychotherapy.

I have a patient-centered approach and involve patients in their care for optimal outcomes. I am a member of American Psychiatric Nurses Association, Association of Advanced Practice Psychiatric Nurses, Clinical TMS Society, and The Brain & Behavior Research Foundation. My goal is to provide high-quality psychiatric and mental health care with empathy and compassion, empowering patients to take charge of their well-being.
